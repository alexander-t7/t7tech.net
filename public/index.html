tech_tips

  • Don't use your ISP's router – it's an underpowered, unsecure nightmare. Put it into bridge mode and deploy your own OpenWrt router.
  • Replace your default DNS servers with these non-logging ones: Mullvad 193.138.218.74 | UncensoredDNS 89.233.43.71 | Cloudflare 1.1.1.1
  • Switch to Firefox; you won't miss Chrome or Safari.
  • Do you work with multiple computers and operating systems simultaneously? The Logitech MX Keys wireless keyboard is a dream come true! Get a Logitech MX Master III wireless mouse and a SteelSeries GCK mousepad to complete the set.
  • As far as monitors go, I recommend anything from the Dell UltraSharp series. Both the 1440p 24/25-inch and 4K 27-inch models are excellent; do yourself a favor and stay away from 1080p for anything bigger than a laptop screen.
  • Need a VPN? Roll your own or use Mullvad.
  • Looking for a web host? Turn to Infomaniak. Use their SwissTransfer service to send big files and kDrive to replace your Dropbox account, or self-host a Nextcloud instance.
  • Back up your data before it's too late! The 3-2-1 method is the best strategy for most people. To automatically back up your files online, give Backblaze a try.
  • Need help with any of the above or want to learn more? Shoot me an email! (link in footer)
